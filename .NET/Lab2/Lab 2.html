Persian

The Persian cat is known for its long hair and sweet nature. They are often quiet and enjoy a calm environment.

Maine Coon

Maine Coons are one of the largest domesticated cat breeds. They are gentle-natured and friendly.

Siamese

Siamese cats are known for their striking features and vocal personality.

Himalayan

Himalayans are a cross between Persian and Siamese cats. They have blue eyes and a gentle nature.
